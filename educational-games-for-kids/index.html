Educational Games for Kids Ages 4–8

Lunelo packs 30 mini-games into one ad-free app, covering letters, numbers, shapes, words and memory. Every game is designed by educators for children ages 4 to 8 — and 6 of them are completely free, no sign-up required.

Learning through play

Young children learn best when they do not realise they are learning. That is the idea behind every game in Lunelo. Instead of worksheets and flashcards, your child traces letters with their finger, counts colourful animals, spots differences between pictures, and builds words by dragging letters into place.

Every game gives immediate feedback through sounds and animations. Correct answers are celebrated. Wrong answers get gentle encouragement — never punishment. There are no timers and no competition against other players. Your child learns at their own pace.

Built for safety

Lunelo takes children's safety seriously. Here is what that means in practice:

  • No ads. Not a single banner, video or interstitial. Children see only educational content.
  • No tracking. We collect zero personal data. No analytics libraries, no advertising SDKs.
  • No social features. No chat, no friend requests, no leaderboards with other users.
  • No surprise purchases. The free games are truly free. Upgrading to all 30 games is a one-time purchase — no subscriptions, no consumables.
  • Works offline. No internet connection needed. Your child's data never leaves the device.
  • COPPA and GDPR compliant. Built from the ground up to meet international privacy standards for children.

Learn more about our safety approach in our safe screen time guide.
